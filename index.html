Sealed is a private two-person chat. All messages are encrypted in your browser with a shared passphrase and exchanged as files. Nothing is sent to any server.

Open the channel

A shared passphrase unlocks the conversation.
It is never stored, never sent anywhere.

How it works. One of you taps Start new, writes a message, then Export to get an encrypted file. Send that file to the other person any way you like. They open it here with the same passphrase, reply, and export it back. The fingerprint in the corner should match on both screens.

No passphrase recovery. Lose it and the messages are unreadable — by anyone, including you. That is the point.
